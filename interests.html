Books

In my free time, I love to read novels. I mostly read fictions, of almost all genres. But I've also enjoyed a few non-fiction or literary fiction books.

I'd like to share 2 of my favorite books, which I think everyone should give a try:

1. How To Kill a Mocking Bird

This book by Harper Lee, follows the childhood of two kids whose lawyer father defends a black man against false accusations. It opened my eye to the harsh reality of racism that is still prevalent in the world.

There's a reason why this book is a winner, made into a movie and included in most school readings. Learn More

2. Homo Sapiens

Homo sapiens was an enlightening and surprisingly pleasant read that took me through a journey about the evolution of human kind, and the impact we as a species have on this planet and it's other inhabitants.

It has helped me gain insight on many things in life that I will now be more mindful of, thanks to this book. Learn More
